How to Take the Quiz

This quiz tests your knowledge of basic web technologies.

You will receive instant feedback after each question.

  1. Click “Start Quiz” to begin.
  2. Read each question carefully.
  3. Select one answer from the options provided.
  4. After selecting an answer, the quiz automatically moves to the next question.
  5. Your score is updated as you progress.
  6. Your final score will be shown at the end of the quiz.
